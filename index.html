The week

A tight cadence from context to board-ready outputs.

Each week is designed to produce usable documents, not a discovery backlog. Workshops are short, evidence is captured, and decisions are traced into the pack.

  1. Day 0 Intake

    Confirm scope, decision horizon, known constraints and stakeholders.

  2. Days 1-2 Evidence capture

    Review existing material, interview decision makers and map obligations.

  3. Day 3 Synthesis

    Draft the principles, requirements, risk and stakeholder artefacts.

  4. Day 4 Challenge

    Run a structured review with sponsors, architecture and delivery leads.

  5. Day 5 Readout

    Hand over the pack, unresolved decisions and recommended next actions.
